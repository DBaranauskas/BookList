About Us

Welcome to Books&More, a vibrant community for book lovers of all kinds! Whether you’re a lifelong reader or just discovering your love for stories, our platform brings together people who share your passion.

📚 Share & Discover Books

Create your personal book list, track what you’ve read, what you’re currently reading, and what’s on your must-read list. Organize your collection, rate your favorites, and write mini-reviews to help others find their next great read.

💬 Join the Conversation

Our Book Talks Forum is a space where readers meet to discuss books, swap recommendations, and dive into thought-provoking debates. Start your own discussion or participate in existing threads — the community is waiting to hear your thoughts.

🌟 Why We’re Different

We combine the fun of a social book club with the organization of a personal library. Your reading journey is more than private — it’s a shared experience. Connect, explore, and grow your love for reading with others who understand your passion.
